Why are we here?

GET SKILLED

With our high-level coaches and exceptional drills, we take skill development seriously. We transform kids into athletes who are ready to play at an elite level.

BE MENTALLY STRONG

Basketball requires more than just athleticism and talent; it requires a strong and healthy mind. Our coaches act as mentors, provide life advice, and help young minds prosper as they grow.

STAY HEALTHY

We stress a healthy lifestyle on and off the court! Through the game itself, conditioning, and healthy lifestyle advice from our coaches, our athletes stay in good shape year-round.

STAY ORGANIZED

We stress proper time management here at the Mavericks. School is top priority! If basketball ever starts to worsen your school grades, we will not let you play.

BE A TEAM PLAYER

On the court and in life, you must be able to help and rely on others. Our athletes learn the importance of teamwork through basketball and create meaningful connections with all their teammates.

GET COMPETITIVE

We create an age-appropriate competitive environment so that all athletes have a chance to play. This allows for everyone to test their newfound skills and improve their playing style.
